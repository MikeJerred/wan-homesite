Background

The client has a B2C ecommerce platform which sells personalised beauty products. After the initial launch the site had a very high bounce rate and low conversion rate, so improving these was the main focus of the project. Also the stakeholders wanted to restructure the way the company sells online.

My Role

I proposed and lead the redesign process. My main task was to improve the layout of the homepage so the product concept is clearly communicated. The second challenge was to simplify the user journey of the existing product customisation feature.

Methods used

Initially, I conducted stakeholder interviews and a design studio to gather business requirements and facilitate team collaboration. I created different versions of wireframes and prototypes based on personas and user stories. I also organised both Guerrilla user testing sessions and remote testing sessions using usertesting.com to validate the designs.
