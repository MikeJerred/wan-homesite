The UX of Death

Background

This is a project under my UX Masters Degree at LCC. The brief was to identify and measure what makes a good death. It was required to prototype a digital intervention which will enhance a positive end of life experience, taking into consideration the last weeks, days and minutes of life.

Challenge

We challenged ourselves to identity a good death for an unborn fetus. Based on research, we discovered stillbirth has a dramatic negative effect on the entire family and the mother especially. However, the bereavement is not always facilitated and supported by wider society. We created an immersive experience to help others understand the pain and sorrow the parents have been through.
