Nested Shapes

Background

Nested Shapes is the project of London-based musician/scientist Peter Jones. Peter previously built a name for himself in the progressive rock world, having been an original member of the progressive metal band Haken, and a current member of jazz fusion supergroup Nova Collective.

Challenge

In collaboration with the artist, I created all the album artworks and the artist profile imagery. I worked closely with him to conceptualise the music in an appropriate visual representation.
