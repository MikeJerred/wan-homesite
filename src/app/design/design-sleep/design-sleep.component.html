Empowering menstrual sleep

Background

Dreem is a technology company created products that monitors, analyzes, and enhance quality of sleep through the use of pink noise. The brief was to develop a product, service, interface, game, programme or physical space that focuses attention on the active outcome of sleep in conjunction with the Dreem products. This project was in collaboration with venturethree and Dreem.

Challenge

Using a range of research methods such as literature review, interviews, and dairy studies, we discovered that many women experience sleep disturbance during menstruation which have an impact on their waking life. Based on the research findings, we designed an interactive VR exhibition to increase awareness around the topic, and integrated new functionalities with the existing Dreem products that benefits this particular customer sector.
