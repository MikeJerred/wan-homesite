Lloyd's Register Foundation HEC

Background

Lloyd's Register Foundation Heritage & Education Centre required a new website in order to support the digital transformation of a large archive of ship reports and documentation.

Challenge

Through stakeholder interviews we were able to understand the desired data structure for the documents and find the balance to organise them efficiently for users. I was responsible for restructuring the website information and creating wireframes, creating interative prototype as well as co-designing the UI.

My Contribution

  • Stakeholder interviews
  • Information architecture
  • Wireframes
  • Interactive prototype with Invision and Sketch Craft
  • UI Design
