The UX of Urban Space

Background

The brief was to observe and map human behaviours in order to create a 3D physical model of an urban space, and to demonstrate digital intervention in the physical space by augmenting the model with technologies.

Challenge

It was an interesting challenge to analyse a large amount of data and find noticeable patterns to present in the model. Influenced by the Starbucks racist scandal, the final outcome was a piece of critical design aimed at creating an exaggerated experience where the viewers are provoked into reflecting upon the affair and discrimination as an issue on a larger scale.
