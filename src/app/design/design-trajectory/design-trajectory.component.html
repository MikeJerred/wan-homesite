Student trajectory app

Background

Masters students at LCC are required to self-evaluate their competency in several categories according to the design competency system. The categories include core skills, such as visualization, and meta competencies such as synthesis. The aim of the project is to replace the current manual process with a mobile app in order to reach a wider audience.

Challenge

In collaboration with the MA UX course leader at LCC and two other students, we used research methods such as participatory design workshop to gather requirements from students.
