Product Customisation - Current site

This is how the product customisation feature works on the current site. There are more than 5 steps before the customer can make a purchase and more than 3 options at each step. The Google Analytics statistics showed a lot of visitors left half way through the process, and some participants from the interviews think there are too many steps and options before they can make a purchase. Therefore, I started to redesign a simplified approach for the product customisation feature.

Prototype for New Product Customisation Feature

After discussing the available options with the product owners, I was able to reduce the number of options, so that the purchase can be completed within two or three steps. I quickly created a low fidelity prototype to demonstrate this simplified approach and validate the assumptions.
