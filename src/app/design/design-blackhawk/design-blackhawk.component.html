Blackhawk Intelligence

Background

The client is a bespoke corporate investigations agency based in London. The company had a very comprehensive offering of services which the old site struggled to present in a structured way.

Challenge

I created a new hierarchical structure to reorganise the content and make service related content more visually distinctive, using different colours to represent each main service category. I also created an area for contextual content on each service page to help users navigate.

I worked on

  • UX Design
  • UI Design
  • Project Management
