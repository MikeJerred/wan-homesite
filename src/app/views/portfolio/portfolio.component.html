Optimise user journey for personalised beauty ecommerce

Background

The client has a B2C ecommerce platform sells personalised beauty products. After the initial launch, the site had a very high bounce rate and low conversion rate. Also the stakeholders wanted to restructure the online sales structure. The requirement was to make appropriate design changes to reduce bounce rate and improve conversion.

My Role

My main task was to simplify and improve the user journey and tie it in to the new sales structure required by the business. The main challenge was to understand the latest business needs and create prototypes and wireframes to validate the concept with users.
